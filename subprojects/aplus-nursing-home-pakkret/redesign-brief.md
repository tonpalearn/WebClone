# Redesign Brief: A PLUS Nursing Home

## Direction

Tone: medical trust / teal-warm
Palette: #0D5C63, #F0B45D, #F7FBF8
Goal: preserve recognizable business category and public facts while making the page feel premium, credible, mobile-first, and easy to contact.

## Priority CTA

LINE @aplusnursinghome / Contact form

## Key Sections

1. Hero with business name, category, and source-backed positioning.
2. Service/admissions/booking journey cards.
3. Source-backed fact table.
4. Contact panel with phone/email/Line/source link.
5. Compliance disclaimer.

## Avoid

- Fake testimonials, prices, awards, medical/education claims.
- Using real logos/images unless separately approved by the business owner.
- Misrepresenting affiliation.
